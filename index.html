Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ONEREQ PRO

Getting Started

How quickly can I get up and running?

Most teams are fully operational within 2-3 business days. We provide onboarding training, data import assistance, and dedicated support to ensure a smooth transition.

Do you offer training for my technicians?

Yes! Every plan includes comprehensive training videos, live webinars, and ongoing support. We also offer on-site training for Enterprise customers.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Pricing & Plans

What's included in each plan?

All plans include Rex AI, parts ordering, surveys, diagnostics, and PDF generation. Higher tiers add more technician accounts, advanced features like commission tracking, and priority support.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there setup fees or contracts?

No setup fees, ever. Plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime.

Technical Details

Does ONEREQ PRO work offline?

Yes! Our mobile app includes offline mode for diagnostics and data collection. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

What devices are supported?

ONEREQ PRO works on desktop (Windows/Mac), tablets (iPad/Android), and smartphones (iOS/Android). Full feature parity across all devices.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL), regular security audits, and SOC 2 compliance. Your data is backed up daily and never shared.

Support & Help

What kind of support do you offer?

Email support for all plans, priority support for Professional/Enterprise, and dedicated account managers for Enterprise. Average response time is under 2 hours.

Do you integrate with my current software?

We integrate with ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, QuickBooks, and many others. Custom integrations available via our API.

Can you help migrate my data?

Yes! Our team will assist with importing your existing customer data, parts lists, and pricing. It's included in onboarding.
